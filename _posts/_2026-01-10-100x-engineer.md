## Executive Summary

10× engineer may have succumbed to inflation, but the 100x engineers have started raising the ceiling again, shipping 5–10× more per week than entire teams shipped per month five years ago.

Very few engineers are taking advantage of the structural leverage created by AI, context saturation, and collapsed coordination costs.

Companies optimized for human coordination (roadmaps, Jira, reviews, QA gates) will not adapt in time.

Winners will be companies that:

- Treat execution as cheap

- Treat context as infrastructure

- Treat AI as an operator, not an assistant
